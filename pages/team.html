superTROOPER95

The inventor of the original "Salamander drop shot" technique. superTROOPER95 is a passionate gamer who has sunk over 2000 hours into the Destiny series and can retell the entirety of the rich Destiny lore to anyone who cares enough to listen. superTROOPER95 will be the first to inform his team members of the latest video game industry news as he looks forward to the teams regular Wednesday night gaming session. Where the team tackles the newest co-operative adventures and latest Xbox Game Pass releases.

Lord Muk

Formely known as rooboy0490, Lord Muk is the oldest member of the team and with age comes wisdom. With the longest history with video games amongst anyone in the team. Lord Muk has gone from conquering Zelda games in his childhood on the Nintendo 64 to slaying the most difficult bosses in games such as Elden Ring. During multiplayer games Lord Muk is generally a competent team member despite the occasional over estimation in his ability which prompts his "If you want me to do everything, just tell me" catchphrase.

Flam1ng N1nja

All great teams have humble beginnings. One of the earliest unions of team members was between Flam1ng N1nja and superTROOPER95 when Flam1ng N1nja snuck into superTROOPER95's DMs asking to play a game of Modern Warfare 2. The power duo has gone on to dominate many Halo and Call of Duty lobbies over the years. Outside of seducing teenage boys, Flam1ng N1nja is a grizzled Halo player who possessed the infamous and coveted gamertag "Flaming Ninja"... until he forgot his password.

Menisel

When there is a game that offers a health gain mechanic you can be sure that Menisel will be along for the ride. Menisel will always be the first to raise his hand to take up the role as healer. Nothing gives the man more satisfaction than healing a fellow team member from 1HP to full health. After overcoming a debilitating World of Warcraft addiction in the 2000's, Menisel has established himself as a Call of Duty veteran sinking countless hours into the long running series.

turtlesak

Arguably the most passionate member of the team. Turtlesak self identifies as a deadeye with what ever the current Call of Duty meta is. With claims of victory when nobody else is around to see. Curious that isn't it? Turtlesak's lineage dates back to his teenage years where he and Menisel purchased an Xbox 360 in secret and had to hide it away from his parents. Playing Halo 3: ODST Firefight at odd hours in the evening just to sneak in a session. Then unplugging and hiding the console away so that his parents wouldn't find out.

xavbeks

Xavbeks or more commonly referred to by his team mates as El Moisto is lethal in the Call of Duty scene. Xavbeks would have the sorest shoulders going around after continuously carrying turtlesak to countless Warzone victories. During the COVID-19 2020 lockdown, Xavbeks was a critical part of Team Salamanders but his recent betrayal in moving to PlayStation coupled with Sony's incompetencies surrounding Cross Play has raised some turmoil within the team.

Ketameme

Ketameme is the newest member of the team. He is a man with a need for speed, you'll typically find him tearing it up in the latest game from the Forumla 1 series. He also has a large number of hours accumulated in Mario Kart 8, where he is lethal on all of the "Big 4" circuits (except for Toad Habour). While his performance in Mario Kart can be questionable at times. Since the launch of Halo Infinite, Ketameme has stepped outside his comfort zone of playing sport and racing games and has greatly improved his first-person shooter skills. Even stepping into GTA V Online where he attends his Marijuana farm.
